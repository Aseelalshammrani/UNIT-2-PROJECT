Alula

Where Ancient Wonders Await

AlUla, an ancient oasis city located in northwestern Saudi Arabia, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a thriving cultural destination. Once a bustling crossroads of civilizations, AlUla's rich history spans millennia, with evidence of human habitation dating back to over 9,000 years.
