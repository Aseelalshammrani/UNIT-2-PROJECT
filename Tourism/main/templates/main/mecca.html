Mecca

The Heart of Islam

Mecca al-Mukarramah, is the holiest city in Islam and is located in western Saudi Arabia. It is the birthplace of the Prophet Muhammad and is the destination for the annual Hajj pilgrimage, which is one of the five pillars of Islam. Millions of Muslims from all over the world travel to Mecca each year to perform the Hajj, which is a spiritual journey that involves circling the Kaaba, praying at the Masjid al-Haram, and walking between the hills of Safa and Marwah.
